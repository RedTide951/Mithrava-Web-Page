A hard-hitting metalcore band from Izmir / Turkey. Mithrava was founded in 2021 by vocalist Oğuzhan Bütüner and lead guitarist Alperen Yıldız. Then Ege Alpat and Arda Çaylı joined as drummer and bassist respectively. The band self-released their first demo, Distances, shortly after their formation.
